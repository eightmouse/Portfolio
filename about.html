02

PASSIONS

  • DESIGN

    I believe design is like a magic wand, if products and services are the magic that solves problems, then design is the tool that helps users truly harness it. Every day, I strive to craft a wand that feels just right in the user's hands.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    Technology has the power to change the world and our everyday lives. As the saying goes, “Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic.” I strive to stay at the forefront of innovation, adapting to new tools and trends. I believe that spreading innovation isn't only the role of engineers, but also a designer's mission.

  • STORY

    I've always loved storytelling, whether in novels, movies, or comics. Stories capture attention, make ideas easier to understand, and help them stick. I believe that by studying different narrative patterns and weaving a sense of story into my own work, I can create designs that truly connect and inspire change.
